Here's how it works.

The system has five modules.

First — your Profile. You tell the system how you think. Your cognitive style, your attention pattern, your strengths and challenges. This becomes your cognitive map.

Second — Emotional Navigator. Log how you feel. Track what triggers you. Discover what helps you regulate. Over time, patterns emerge.

Third — Sensory Check-In. Rate your sensory load across five channels. See how your environment affects you.

Fourth — Discoveries. Capture insights as they come. A realization. A pattern you noticed. A question worth exploring.

Fifth — Pattern Engine. The system finds cycles you might miss. Overwhelm loops. Flow triggers. Burnout precursors. And it asks the inverted question: what states are missing from your map?

Everything stays on your machine. No cloud. No tracking. Your mind, mapped quietly.
