Let's be clear about what this is not.

This is not a productivity tool. It will not help you do more. It will not optimize your morning routine or gamify your habits.

This is cognitive infrastructure.

It helps you understand how you think. How you regulate. How you enter flow. How you burn out. How you recover.

The goal is not to produce more. The goal is to understand yourself well enough that you stop fighting your own brain.
