Getting started takes about three minutes.

First, you'll set up your profile. How do you think? How does your attention move? What are your strengths and challenges?

This isn't a test. There are no right answers. It's just the starting point for your cognitive map.

Then you'll do your first check-in. How are you feeling right now? What's your energy? Your sensory load?

That's it. You've started.

From here, you check in when you remember. Log emotions when they're strong. Capture insights when they come.

The system does the rest. Patterns emerge. Guidance appears. Your map fills in.

There's no wrong way to use this. Just show up when you can.
