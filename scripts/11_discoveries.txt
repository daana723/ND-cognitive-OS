The Discoveries module is for the moments that slip away.

The realization at 2am. The pattern you noticed in the shower. The connection between two things that shouldn't be related but are.

You open it. You capture it. You tag it if you want.

Insight. Pattern. Realization. Question. Breakthrough. There are five types. Pick the closest one.

Over time, your discoveries become the richest part of your cognitive map.

Not because the system analyzes them. Because you can see your own thinking evolve.

Your discoveries, collected. Your mind, documented.

This is what a second brain should actually be.
