Emotional regulation is not about feeling less. It's about understanding more.

Why am I anxious right now? What triggered this? What usually helps? How intense is this, really?

These questions seem simple. But most people never systematically ask them.

The Emotional Navigator makes it a habit. Quick. Low-effort. Private.

And after a few months, you know things about your emotional patterns that most people never learn.

You know that you're most regulated after movement. That interrupted sleep precedes overwhelm by exactly one day. That certain environments trigger anxiety predictably.

This isn't therapy. This is self-knowledge. Built from your own data. At your own pace.
